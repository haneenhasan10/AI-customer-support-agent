We prioritize secure transactions. Customers can pay using the following methods:

Credit/Debit Cards: Visa and Mastercard are accepted.

Digital Wallets: Apple Pay is fully supported for mobile checkout.

Standard Shipping: Takes 2 to 3 business days for major cities.

Extended Areas: May take up to 5 business days.

We offer a strict but fair refund policy to maintain service quality:

Late Requests: Any request made after 5 days (e.g., 6 or 10 days) will be automatically rejected.
